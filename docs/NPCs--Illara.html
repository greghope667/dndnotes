Illara


Commoner in Arinsguard. Young half-elf woman. Flowy dark hair. Actually spelled Elara.

We found Illara's body in Nathaniel's shop?

Following Nathaniel's directions, we found her trapped under a tree in the woods? As an undead?
We freed her and she attacked us.

Had some sort of beauty amulet to hide her being undead?

Amulet has some memories or essence of Illara. She thinks it is Ches 4th (15 days ago), no memory since then.

Hypothesis: The amulet contains Illara's essence, projecting it onto the undead body.

In the cave, she recognised a silver ring from Trand.

After removing the amulet from her for some time, she stopped being undead.

We buried her (in a shallow grave) in the woods.
