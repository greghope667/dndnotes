Illaras Amulet


Found on Illara when we found her near Blackwater Lake.

When she wore the neckless, she looked fine and seemed herself. When she took it of, she became a mindless zombie.

Bonri wore the neckless, he took damage and was unable to remove it.
Trying to attack the amulet to free him caused a burst of energy in the stone, releasing another ghost to attack us.

We cast identify on the amulet:
- Something is resisting the identification
- It is a cursed magic item
- Standard remove curse spell would remove the item from Bonri
- Item was created by a high level spell
- Like an exchange point for energy, someone using this transfer to release beings into the world
- Bonri's book said we should keep the amulet, don't know where the other end of the transfer is

The Duchess was able to easily remove the necklace from Bonri, unlike anyone else.
