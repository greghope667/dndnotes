A Captive's Request


Ches 19, Night, Arinsguard

We questioned Nathanial in William's presence.

He is due to be taken to Zoscara to face justice for his alleged crimes.

Nathanial secretly gave us a map to a location near Blackwater Lake.

Following the map, we found an area of dead trees in the forest.
We heard Illara crying for help, trapped under a fallen tree. We freed her and she attacked us.

Illara turned out to be some kind of undead creature.

It appears Illara has been dead (or no memory) for some time, since Ches 4 (15 days ago).
She appears to have an amulet that projects her form onto an undead zombie body.

We spent some time exploring a cave nearby, finding some treasure.

Ches 20, Daytime

We investigated the amulet and stone. Bonri wore the amulet, and we were attacked by a ghost again.

Heading back to Arinsguard, we found Nathaniel had been taken to Zoscara by fast riding horses.

We rode to Zoscara.

Ches 21, Afternoon

Guards stopped to search carriages into city. They tried to take our magical items, we persuaded them not to.

We went to the Temple of Tier, turns out our party member (Icarus) is a member. We slept overnight in the temple.
