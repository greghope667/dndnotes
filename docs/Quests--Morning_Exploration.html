Morning Exploration


Ches 22, (Early) Morning

Bonri and I decided to explore Zoscara together and investigate the necklace.

The necklace seemed to drain more life from Bonri overnight.

We went to the archive in Zoscara. They refused to let us in without a permit.

We sold our loot from the cave from some gold.
The jeweller mentioned Lady Tenika Greytales had had some earings stolen, we could investigate.

Someone gave us a note secretly - “She will meet you, tonight, sunset, the well”
