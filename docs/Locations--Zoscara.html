Zoscara


Largest city in Elyria.

City on the side of a valley.

Built on two levels, upper and lower level are upper and lower class.

White-grey stone castle in upper tier.

Fast flowing waterfalls through city.

To the west, a large river flows out towards the sea.

Old city, existed a while.

Contains Temple of Tier and Temple of Selune.

Large sign saying ‘NO MAGIC’.

No casting magic, but some magical items okay.

In the temples, magical enchantments:
Tier: No lying, stealing, invisibility
Selune: Invisible ceiling

Archives: Needs gold pass to enter from head scholar.
Medium large, white stone building, next to Baron's castle.
